The Foundation

The Problem

The model looks right on screen — then the print comes out the wrong size. For a newer maker that miss is common, and it isn't free:

  • • Wasted filament on a print you can't use
  • • Hours of machine time gone
  • • You only find out after it's printed

The Solution

Check scale in the real world before you print.

  • • Upload your model
  • • Set its real print dimensions
  • • See it at true 1:1 scale through your phone camera
  • • Decide: does it fit, does it look right?

TrueScale closes the gap between the screen and the shelf — markerless camera AR, no extra hardware, just the phone in your pocket.
